Our Product

BuildinX thrives to become the markt leader in the handling of component-oriented processes, data and information. Our platform integrates every step of the entire value chain and lifecycle of a component.

Auto-ID

Components are assigned a marker with an unique ID which is auto-generated by the platform and can be attached to the component on the construction site.

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

We leverage the current and future-proof standard BIM which aims to digitize the construction site and create digital twins of objects and resources.

Lean

User-centric implementation yields overall lean processes.

Lifecycle

Project-agnostic lifecycle management of components.

Logistics

Digital component-twins aid to identify and solve logistic gaps.

Tracking

Near-time component state information on demand.
